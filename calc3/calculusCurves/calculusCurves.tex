In this section we differentiate and integrate curves in space.
\end{abstract}

\maketitle





The derivative of the curve in $\R^3$ ,
\[
\vec r(t) = \vector{f(t), g(t), h(t)}
\]
is given by
\[
\vec r\, '(t) = \vector{f'(t), g'(t), h'(t)}
\]

Note that this ia a vector equation.
